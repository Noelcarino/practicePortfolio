About

My first exposure to programming was in college of 2014. I was attending Long Beach City College at the time and they offered an Hour of Code session for anyone interested in trying out programming. The professor had us develop a Flappy Bird android game, using puzzles that represented blocks of code. It was at this moment, I knew that coding is something that I want to pursue. I have always wanted to develop rhythm games but not having the knowledge made that possibility feel worlds apart. Fast forward to today, I have attended an Intensive Web Development Bootcamp called LearningFuze. It was here that I honed my skills in Web Development that has given me the confidence to develop and contribute to developing websites and programs.
One aspect of programming that is appealing to me is that it can be a teacher in itself. It has taught me not to rush into building things right away and the importance of planning Without a vision of the end goal, programming can be very difficult.
The process of programming and being able to share it with my peers is something that helps me keep going. I love being able to share the tough moments in programming with others because it makes the journey to becoming a better programmer. It is also a reminder that I am not alone in wanting to become the best developer I can be.
My biggest triumph in becoming a developer is developing my first Full Stack LAMP React E-Commerce application. I highly value this project because completing it gave me the realization that I have the ability to develop a fully functioning application.
